## Standalone Server

Nodepilot helps in provisioning a standalone node on the platform of your choice.

The following is the estimated cost for running a [**recommended configuration Sui blockchain node**](https://docs.sui.io/build/fullnode#hardware-requirements) on popular platform providers in Americas region.

As per the official documentation for a Sui full node its recommended to have minimum of the following:

- CPU: 8 cores or 16 vCPUs

### Base

According to the official [documentation](https://docs.base.org/guides/run-a-base-node#hardware-requirements) for a Base full node its recommended to have an SSD of 1TB but
we recommend to have atleast 4TB SSD for downloading and restoring snapshots. As per writing this doc each mainnet snapshot takes around 1.5TB of space. So here are our recommendations:

- CPU: 4 cores or 8 vCPUs
- Memory: 16 GB
- Disk: 4 TB SSD
