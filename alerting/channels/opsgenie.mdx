---
title: OpsGenie
description: Steps to setup alerts for OpsGenie
---
Click on **Integrations** tab on the left, then press **Connect** under **OpsGenie**.
![](/images/ops-genie-alert-1.png)
Enter your **API Key** from your **OpsGenie** account. Click on the link for instructions.
![](/images/ops-genie-api-key.png)

Verify by seeing **Connected** under the **OpsGenie** section in the Integrations tab.
![](/images/ops-genie-alert-2.png)


**Note: If linked is clicked, look for API Key Management Tab section**
![](/images/ops-genie-key-manage.png)
