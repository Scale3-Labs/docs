---
title: Webhook
description: Steps to setup alerts for Webhook
---
 Click on **Integrations** tab on the left, then press **Connect** under **Webhook**.


![](/images/alert-channel-webhook-1.png)

Enter your Webhook URL and click Resister. 
![](/images/alert-channel-webhook-2.png)
Verify by seeing **Connected** under the **Webhook** section in the Integrations tab.
![](/images/alert-channel-webhook-3.png)
