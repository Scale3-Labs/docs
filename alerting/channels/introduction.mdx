title: Setup
description: Alert Channels allow you to route alerts to one of more channels of your choice. We currently support the following channels
---

1. On the Home Page, click the **Settings** tab. 
![](/images/email-alert-channel.png)

2. Click on **Integrations** tab on the left to see the available alert channels.
![](/images/slack-alert-channel-1.png)

- [Email](/alerting/channels/email)
- [Slack](/alerting/channels/slack)
- [PagerDuty](/alerting/channels/pagerduty)
- [OpsGenie](/alerting/channels/opsgenie)
- [Telegram](/alerting/channels/telegram)
- [Webhook](/alerting/channels/webhook)
