---
title: PagerDuty
description: Steps to setup alerts for PagerDuty
---

1. On the Home Page, click the Settings tab.
![](/images/email-alert-channel.png)
2. Click on **Integrations** tab on the left, then press **Connect** under **PagerDuty**.
![](/images/pager-duty-alert-1.png)
3. Enter your **Integration Key** from your **PagerDuty** account. Click on the link for instructions.
![](/images/pager-duty-alert-2.png)
4. Enter your Integration Key and press **Connect** at the bottom 

5. Verify by seeing **Connected** under the **PagerDuty** section in the **Integrations** tab.
![](/images/pager-duty-alert-3.png)
6. All Done.

 Note: If linked is clicked, look for Generate a New Integration Key. 
 ![](/images/generate-key.png)
