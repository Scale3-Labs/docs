---
title: PagerDuty
description: Steps to setup alerts for PagerDuty
---
 Click on **Integrations** tab on the left, then press **Connect** under **PagerDuty**.
![](/images/pager-duty-alert-1.png)
Enter your **Integration Key** from your **PagerDuty** account. Click on the link for instructions.
![](/images/pager-duty-alert-2.png)
 Enter your Integration Key and press **Connect** at the bottom 

Verify by seeing **Connected** under the **PagerDuty** section in the **Integrations** tab.
![](/images/pager-duty-alert-3.png)


 **Note: If linked is clicked, look for Generate a New Integration Key.**
 ![](/images/generate-key.png)
