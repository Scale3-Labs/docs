1. Click on  **Validators** from the top panel. Then click on Alerts Policies, then click on **Create Alert**
![](/images/validatorsalerts.png)

2. Select Protocol then enter validator public key

![](/images/createalertkey.png)

3. Add alert channels if not configured already. See [Alert Channels](/channels/introduction) section on how to manage alert channels. Once your channels are added successfully you will see **Channel** section.



4. Finally, select the alerts you want to subscribe to by clicking the circles on the right side.
![](/images/createalertkey1.png)
> **Note:** You will only be able to subscribe to channels that are connected otherwise the circle will be greyed out.
