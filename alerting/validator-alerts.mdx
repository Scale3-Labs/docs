1. Click on **Onchain Alerts** from the side panel. Then click on **Manage Alerts**

![](/images/alert-flow-instr-4.png)

2. Choose your **Validator** from by either scrolling through the dropdown or searching by name or address

![](/images/alert-flow-instr-5.png)

3. Add alert channels if not configured already. See [Alert Channels](/channels/introduction) section on how to manage alert channels. Once your channels are added successfully you will see **Connected** in the status column.

![](/images/alert-flow-instr-6.png)

4. Finally select the alerts you want to subscribe to by clicking the checkboxes
> **Note:** You will only be able to subscribe to channels that are connected otherwise the checkbox will be greyed out.

![](/images/alert-flow-instr-7.png)
