Email Channel Setup 

1. Click the settings button from the side panel and click **Manage your account**
![](/images/alert-flow-instr-1.png)

2. Click on **Alert Channels**

![](/images/alert-flow-instr-2.png)

3. Add your **email** by entering it in the input dialog and click the **Add** button. 

![](/images/alert-flow-instr-3.png)

**Voila**! You are all setup to subscribe to email alerts.
