Email Channel Setup 
1. On the Home Page, click the **Settings** tab. 
![](/images/email-alert-channel.png)
2. Click on **Integrations** tab on the left, then press **Manage** under **Email**.
![](/images/email-alert-channel-2.png)
![](/images/email-alert-channel-3.png)
3. Manage Emails provide a space to input email addresses 
![](/images/email-alert-channel-4.png)
4. Click on Add Email, once email is added it will show
![](/images/email-alert-channel-5.png)
**Voila**! You are all setup to subscribe to email alerts.
