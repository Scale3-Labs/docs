---
title: Slack
description: Steps to setup alerts for Slack
---

1. On the Home Page, click the **Settings** tab. 
![](/images/email-alert-channel.png)

2. Click on **Integrations** tab on the left, then press **Connect** under **Slack**.
![](/images/slack-alert-channel-1.png)


3. You will be redirected in order to enter your Slack workspace
![](/images/slack-alert-channel-2.png)
4. Allow permissions and select a Slack Channel, then click allow

![](/images/slack-alert-channel-3.png)
**All done**
