---
title: Telegram
description: Steps to setup alerts for Telegram
---
1. On the Home Page, click the Settings tab.
![](/images/email-alert-channel.png)
2. Click on **Integrations** tab on the left, then press **Connect** under **Telegram**.
![](/images/telegram-alert-1.png)
3. On screen instructions will show how to connect your Chat ID
![](/images/telegram-chat-id-1.png)

![](/images/telegram-chat-id-2.png)
4. Enter Chat ID and click connect 

5. Verify by seeing **Connected** under the **Telegram** section in the Integrations tab.

![](/images/telegram-chat-id-3.png)
