---
title: OpsGenie
description: Steps to setup alerts for OpsGenie
---

1. On the Home Page, click the Settings tab.
![](/images/email-alert-channel.png)
2. Click on **Integrations** tab on the left, then press **Connect** under **OpsGenie**.
![](/images/ops-genie-alert-1.png)
3. Enter your **API Key** from your **OpsGenie** account. Click on the link for instructions.
![](/images/ops-genie-api-key.png)

4. Verify by seeing **Connected** under the **OpsGenie** section in the Integrations tab.
![](/images/ops-genie-alert-2.png)
5. All Done

Note: If linked is clicked, look for API Key Management Tab section 
![](/images/ops-genie-key-manage.png)
